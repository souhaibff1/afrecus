Community Rules

1

Respect everyone

Treat all community members with respect and kindness, regardless of their background, beliefs, or gaming skills.

2

No hate speech or harassment

Hate speech, harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks will not be tolerated in any form.

3

Keep discussions positive

Maintain a positive and constructive atmosphere in all conversations and interactions within the community.

4

Follow platform rules

Adhere to Twitch, YouTube, and other platform rules during streams and interactions on those platforms.

5

No spam or self-promotion

Avoid spamming, excessive self-promotion, or posting irrelevant links without permission from moderators.

6

Moderator discretion

Moderators have the final say in rule enforcement. Violations may result in warnings, timeouts, or bans.
